Coffee

Description

Homemade version of Starbucks' classic.

Ingrediants
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons hot chocolate powder
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ¾ cup brewed coffee
  • 2 ½ tablespoons caramel sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • Whipped cream
Steps
  1. Brew coffee.
  2. While waiting for coffee to brew, microwave milk until bubbling.
  3. Combine brewed coffee and hot milk in a mug.
  4. Add hot chocolate powder, ½ teaspoon sea salt and 2 tablespoons caramel sauce. Stir to combine.
  5. Top with whipped cream, sugar, ½ teaspoon sea salt and ½ tablespoon caramel sauce.
